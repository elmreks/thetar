# TAR — General Pitch

The Technical Achievement Registry (TAR) is a parody institution that certifies absurd accomplishments.

The humor comes from treating ridiculous events with the seriousness of a government agency.

Examples include:
- Most Dick Pics Sent in One Day
- Most Dramatically Consumed Banana in a Bar
- Longest Distance on Skateboard After Partner Bailed

TAR can become a participatory community where people submit technically valid achievements.
